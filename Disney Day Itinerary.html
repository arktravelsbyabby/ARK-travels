Disney Day Itinerary

🎉 Disney Day!! 🎉

(With DAS Pass – Curated Based on the Client's Preferred Rides)

Note:Even if you book a ride for a specific time with the DAS pass, you can ride it anytimeafter that time. For example, if you booked at 11:00 AM, you may ride any time after 11:00 AM. However, you cannot book another ride until the current one is redeemed (ridden) or canceled.

Take breaks as needed, enjoy less crowded rides in between, and follow this itinerary at your own pace!
8:30 AM – Depart from Hotel
9:00–9:15 AM – Security Check + Ferry to Magic Kingdom Entrance
9:20 AM – Meet Mickey & Minnie at Town Square Theater
→ Use DAS if wait times are high
10:00 AM – Board Main Street Locomotive to Cinderella Castle
DAS Booking Suggestion:Choose one to begin with
  • Seven Dwarfs Mine Train
  • The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh
  • Peter Pan’s Flight
  • It’s a Small World
10:00–10:30 AM – Photo Session in front of the Castle
→ Catch the Disney Adventure Friends Cavalcade Parade
10:45 AM – Mad Tea Party
→ Seven Dwarfs Mine Train reserved for 11:15 AM
11:15 AM – Seven Dwarfs Mine Train
→ Booked: The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh (12:00 PM)
11:30–11:50 AM – Restroom/Snack Break at Cosmic Ray’s Starlight Café
12:00 PM – The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh
→ Booked: Tomorrowland Speedway (12:30 PM)
12:15 PM – Character Photo with Pooh & Tigger
12:30 PM – Tomorrowland Speedway
→ Book Buzz Lightyear or PeopleMover depending on crowd
→ Also reserve: Tiana’s Bayou Adventure, after these rides or directly if lines are short for both Buzz and PeopleMover
💡 Tip: If Buzz or PeopleMover are crowded, try Carousel of Progress (indoor, relaxing).
1:00 PM – Buzz Lightyear’s Space Ranger Spin
→ Booked: Tiana’s Bayou Adventure (2:20 PM)
1:30 PM – Tomorrowland Transit Authority PeopleMover
1:45 PM – Dumbo the Flying Elephant
2:00 PM – Under the Sea – Journey of The Little Mermaid
☔ Weather Tip: If it rains, go to Carousel of Progress, Tiki Room, or Hall of Presidents.
2:15 PM – Catch the Walt Disney World Railroad at Fantasyland → Travel to Frontierland
2:45 PM – Tiana’s Bayou Adventure
→ Booked: Peter Pan’s Flight (3:20 PM)
3:00–3:15 PM – Bathroom + Walk to Peter Pan’s Flight
3:20 PM – Peter Pan’s Flight
→ Booked: It’s a Small World (4:40 PM)
3:30–4:30 PM – Rest & Meal Break at Pinocchio Village Haus
→ Use Mobile Ordering
→ Additional seating available near restrooms and Peter Pan’s Flight
⏳ Early? Fill time with Mickey’s PhilharMagic nearby.
4:40 PM – It’s a Small World
→ Booked: Pirates of the Caribbean (5:20 PM)
5:20 PM – Pirates of the Caribbean
→ Booked: The Magic Carpets of Aladdin (6:00 PM)
5:30–6:00 PM – Relax at the Enchanted Tiki Room
6:00 PM – Magic Carpets of Aladdin
→ Booked: Jungle Cruise (6:45 PM)
6:45 PM – Jungle Cruise

🌟 Evening Fireworks

Time: 10:00 PM
Recommended Arrival: 9:00 PM at Castle Front

🎈 Option to Exit and Return: You may leave after Jungle Cruise and re-enter for fireworks.

🚂 Take the Railroad from Frontierland to return to Main Street

🍽️ Dining: Check recommended locations if staying for fireworks

⚠️ Reminder: Expect heavy crowds after fireworks; plan rideshare pickups accordingly.
